Welcome to a Pueblo-enhanced TinyMUSH.

<UL>
<LI> "connect <name> <password>" connects you to an existing character.
<LI> "create <name> <password>" creates a new character.
<LI> "WHO" tells you who is logged in to the game (case sensitive).
<LI> "QUIT" exits the game and saves your character.
<LI> "news" informs you about recent program changes and items of interest.
<LI> "help" gives help on the commands, "help commands" for a list.
</UL>
